HomeTeam GameDev Club Contributions

About HT GD Club

HT GD Club is a place where members of the club make games together as a team and without any kind of stress or crunch.

The club has friendly collaborators and experienced mentors that help finish bigger projects on time. The club have made more than 80 games over the past 4 years.

We make original games and everyone can pitch a game idea to start a new game project. I joined HT GD Club on Febuary 2018 and ever since then, I have contributed to 7 games.

My experience with HT GD Club is so positive. Not only I learned new skills but it also gave me confidence to make even better games.
